Portfolio > Projects > L2J Private Server

Informações

This was an experience I had as an administrator of an online MMORPG game called Lineage II, this is a game emulated in java in the High Five chronicle, where players are born in the cities of their respective races (Human, Elf, Dark Elf, Orc, Dwarf and Kamael) and should seek to increase their power level through Skills, buffs, Weapons, Armor and Jewels by conquering Strongholds and Castles with their clan. Most of the private servers are focused on PvP (Player vs Player), where the player takes a level easily but it takes a while to make your character 100% complete, the biggest difference of this project was the balanced balance so that it wasn't so easy to make your character complete character and at the same time bringing the nostalgia of doing things the old fashioned way looking for NPCs where they need the items instead of a central NPC that does everything, another big difference was the NPCs' lines and translated quests to encourage the player to do it the important missions of the game for the evolution of your character.
In this project I had to manage all the content of the project alone, in addition to managing the monetization through the tools of the website itself, it was also necessary to fix bugs in the datapack and in the game client quickly, I also managed the protection of the server, the website and the database. game data, experiencing difficulties such as
- "What can I sell that will make the player buy again without making the player who doesn't spend money feel wronged?"
- "How to make events that attract new players to the game using items that seem to bring him an advantage but that do not affect the character to the point that he has an advantage over other players?"
